Account deletion request

Delete your Outfit Checkers account

You can request deletion of your Outfit Checkers account and related personal data by contacting us using the email address below.

Important security requirement

For account security, deletion requests must be sent from the same email address linked to your Outfit Checkers account.

Requests sent from a different email address may not be processed unless account ownership can be verified.

How to request account deletion

To request deletion of your Outfit Checkers account, please send an email from the same email address linked to your Outfit Checkers account.

Email subject: Delete my Outfit Checkers account

Please include the following information in your request:

  • Your Outfit Checkers account email
  • Your Outfit Checkers username
  • A clear statement that you want your account deleted

For security reasons, we may ask for additional information to verify that the request belongs to the account owner.

Contact support

Processing time

Account deletion requests are generally processed within 30 days after we verify that the request was sent by the account owner or that account ownership has been confirmed.

Data that may be deleted or anonymized

When your account deletion request is processed, the following account-related data may be deleted or anonymized where technically and legally possible:

  • Account and profile information
  • Profile photo and uploaded outfit photos
  • Posts and post-related content
  • Comments and ratings linked to your account
  • Account-related social interaction data

Data that may be retained

Some records may be retained for a limited period where required for security, abuse prevention, legal obligations, subscription or payment verification, dispute resolution, fraud prevention, or platform integrity.

Subscriptions

Deleting the app or requesting account deletion may not automatically cancel an active subscription. If you have an active subscription, please cancel it through Google Play or the App Store.
